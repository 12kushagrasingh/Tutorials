Matplotlib is 2D and 3D plotting library.
It can produce high quality graphs.
It also supports animations also.

Developed by American neurobiologist, John D. Hunter
He wrote this in python and was released in 2003.

Graphs it supports:
1. Histogram
2. Bar Charts
3. Scatterplots
4. Area Plot
5. Pie Plot
6. Error Charts
7. Power Spectra

It depends on the NumPy library to work.

for more: https://matplotlib.org/2.0.2/Matplotlib.pdf
